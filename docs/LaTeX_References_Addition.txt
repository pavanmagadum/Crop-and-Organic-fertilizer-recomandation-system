SAMPLE PARAGRAPH FOR YOUR METHODOLOGY SECTION
===============================================================================

Add this to your paper's Methodology section:

\subsection{Organic Fertilizer Mapping}

The system includes a novel organic fertilizer recommendation module that 
converts conventional synthetic fertilizers to organic alternatives based on 
nutrient equivalency analysis. Our mapping database was developed through 
comprehensive review of organic farming literature \cite{martin1992, 
solomon2012, ramamoorthy2015, magdoff2021, brady2016}, combining scientific 
nutrient composition data with traditional Indian organic farming practices 
\cite{palekar2006, icar2015, ncof2019}.

For each synthetic fertilizer (e.g., Urea, DAP, MOP), we identified 2-3 
organic alternatives with comparable nutrient profiles. The conversion ratios 
were calculated considering: (i) NPK content of organic materials 
\cite{solomon2012, magdoff2021}, (ii) nutrient release rates 
\cite{brady2016}, and (iii) bioavailability in soil \cite{brady2016, 
magdoff2021}. For example, Urea (46\% N) is mapped to Jivamrutha, 
Vermicompost Extract, or Compost Tea, with conversion ratios adjusted for 
the slow-release nature and microbial benefits of organic sources 
\cite{palekar2006, sharma2017, magdoff2021}.

Each organic alternative includes detailed preparation instructions compiled 
from established organic farming literature \cite{martin1992, rodale1971, 
palekar2006} and validated through government agricultural extension 
resources \cite{icar2015, ncof2019}. The system provides step-by-step 
preparation guides to ensure farmer-friendly implementation.
