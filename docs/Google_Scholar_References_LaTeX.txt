===============================================================================
HOW TO USE THESE REFERENCES
===============================================================================

1. ADD TO YOUR BIBLIOGRAPHY:
   Copy the \bibitem{} entries above and paste them into your LaTeX file
   before \end{thebibliography}

2. CITE IN YOUR PAPER:
   Use \cite{} commands in your text. Examples:

   "Vermicompost has been shown to contain 1.5-2\% nitrogen and improve
   soil fertility \cite{sinha2010, pathma2012, yadav2013}."

   "Traditional Indian organic practices such as Jivamrutha have been
   documented in agricultural research \cite{devakumar2014, palekar2006zbnf}."

   "Compost tea preparation methods have been standardized for nutrient
   extraction \cite{ingham2005, scheuerell2002}."

   "Studies demonstrate that vermicompost can effectively replace chemical
   fertilizers while maintaining crop yields \cite{arancon2004, atiyeh2002,
   pant2009}."
